### 1. What Are Cookies
Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our site

They help us recognize your browser, remember preferences, and understand how visitors use Altiora

### 2. Types of Cookies We Use
- **Essential cookies**
  Required for the site to function, for example to load pages securely and manage your waitlist signup

- **Analytics cookies**
  Help us understand traffic, measure engagement, and improve the site using tools like Google Analytics or similar services

- **Preference cookies**
  Allow us to remember your settings such as language or region when available

### 3. How We Use Cookies
We use cookies to

- Enable core site functionality
- Improve site performance and reliability
- Track visitor activity in aggregate for analytics
- Personalize the experience when features expand

### 4. Managing Cookies
You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings

Most browsers allow you to block cookies or alert you when cookies are being set, please note that disabling cookies may affect how the site functions

### 5. Third Party Cookies
Some cookies may be placed by trusted third parties that provide analytics or technical services

These third parties are required to handle data in accordance with privacy standards
